Analytic Tool for Real Estate Agency

The Mission

My mission was to design a set of analytic tool which helps them to make business decisions.

Scale

5 people ｜ 6 months+
from 0 to 1

Client

Newland (新聯陽實業機構)

Website

Visit the website

Stakeholders Interview

We met stakeholders 1-2 per week. The project owners gave me mostly information about how target users used to do their job, what they care, who influences them. I also gave them feedback about what their user really do after some data research. Sometime, the topic are too far from the project owner, he invites the crucial stakeholder to the meeting.

Initial Research & Discover

After I’ve learned the company cultural, stakeholders expectation, and target users’ workflow, I concluded them into scenarios the analytic tool could be useful in. Fortunately, the project owners really like my proposal.

In-depth Study

Each scenario represents a topic. I spent averagely 2 weeks on researching on 1 topic. For instance, the project owner said the salespeople used to decide their media strategy with their guts and experience, not knowing which media is the most effective way to attract potential buyers. The topic I research is salespeople’s media strategy decision flow, including how often they iterates the proportion, what tools they use to communicate.
